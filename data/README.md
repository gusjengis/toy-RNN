TinyStories sample
==================

`tinystories_sample.txt` is a 2 MiB byte-range sample from the TinyStories training split:

https://huggingface.co/datasets/roneneldan/TinyStories

It is intended for early character-level RNN experiments where a small, readable English corpus is more useful than a large dataset. Stories are separated by the `<|endoftext|>` marker from the original dataset.
